Deforgify

Deforgify is a tool that utilizes the power of Deep Learning to distinguish Real images from the Fake ones. For instance, if someone takes your original image and inserts your face into a murder scene or photoshops it onto someone else's body, Deforgify will tag it as fake reducing the chances of it being used to smear you.
